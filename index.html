Welcome to Reimagine 6th Street!

"Reimagine 6th Street" is a project to repurpose 6th Street in Koreatown from Normandie Ave. to Vermont Ave. for a public pedestrian plaza to support existing Koreatown community members and stakeholders. The goals of this project are three-fold: to provide open-space in Koreatown, reduce traffic violence, and boost existing small businesses.

Reimagining 6th Street

"Reimagine 6th Street" is a project to repurpose 6th Street in Koreatown from Normandie Ave. to Vermont Ave. for a public pedestrian plaza to support existing Koreatown community members and stakeholders. The goals of this project are three-fold: to provide open-space in Koreatown, reduce traffic violence, and boost existing small businesses.

"Reimagine 6th Street"는 한인타운의 6번가에 (Normandie Ave.과 Vermont Ave. 사이) 공공 보행자 광장을 조성하여 기존 한인타운 커뮤니티 구성원과 관계자를 지원하는프로젝트입니다. 이 프로젝트의 목표는 한인타운에 열린 공간을 제공하고 자동차 사고를 줄이며 기존 중소기업을 돕는 것입니다.

“Reimaginar 6th St” es un proyecto para reutilizar 6th St en Koreatown de Normandie Ave. a Vermont Av. para una plaza peatonal pública para apoyar a los miembros de la comunidad de Koreatown y a las partes interesadas existentes. Los objetivos de este proyecto son tres: proporcionar espacios abiertos en Koreatown, reducir la violencia de tráfico e impulsar las pequeñas empresas existentes.
